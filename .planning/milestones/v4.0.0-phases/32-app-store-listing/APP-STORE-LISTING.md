## App Review Notes

ValetudiOS requires a Valetudo robot vacuum on the local network to function. Valetudo is open-source firmware for robot vacuums (https://valetudo.cloud).

**Testing without a robot:**
The app will show the robot list screen. Without a Valetudo device on the network, the scanner will find no devices. The app's UI, navigation, settings, and support views are fully functional without a connected robot.

**Testing with a robot:**
If you have access to a Valetudo instance, add it via hostname/IP. The app will connect and show the live map, controls, and status.

**Network requirement:**
The app communicates exclusively over the local network (LAN). It does not connect to any external servers. The "Local Network" permission prompt is required for operation.

**No login required:**
The app does not require any account or registration. Robot credentials (if configured on the Valetudo instance) are entered directly in the app.
